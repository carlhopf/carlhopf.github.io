Dominoes PlayDrift

Dominoes is one of the most popular classic board games in the world. Your aim is to match tiles and score the most points. Play online and challenge your friends in Muggins, Draw or Block.

This project first was an experiment in cross-platform html5 development, but later on grew into a fully featured game ontop of a homegrown multiplayer platform.

Available for Web, Android, iOS, Chrome, Windows and Facebook.
